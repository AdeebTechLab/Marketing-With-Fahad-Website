Our Story

Marketing with Fahad began with a clear opportunity: businesses across the UAE wanted an online presence, but very few specialists existed who truly understood both web development and search engine visibility. We built the agency to close that gap.

Our first projects used popular CMS platforms, always with original, custom design. Soon it became clear that building a website was only half the job — the other half was making sure people could find it. That insight pushed us to focus deeply on search engine algorithms from the very beginning.

In 2008 we officially launched Marketing with Fahad as a dedicated SEO and digital marketing agency. Our proprietary CMS platform launched alongside the agency and has been improving ever since — now powering everything from corporate websites to full e-commerce stores, all built to rank.
