The theory of social networks is known to everyone — Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, TikTok, LinkedIn. Social Media Marketing (SMM) in Dubai is a relatively new but very effective service for businesses looking to grow their audience and revenue.

Optimization

Social Media Optimization & Promotion

The primary goal of SMM is to attract customer attention to your company through social networks. The main purpose is to increase traffic by attracting social network users. This type of promotion is becoming very popular due to the increasing influence of social platforms and the declining efficiency of classical internet marketing methods.

The big social networks have enormous audiences. For example, there are almost 90 million people registered on Google+ alone. Missing the opportunity to attract this audience is a significant mistake for any growing business.

If your primary purpose is to sell goods or services through the Internet, it is essential to have positive feedback on your website. More good reviews on blogs, forums, communities and social networks give you a higher chance that people will buy your product.

According to statistics, social networks can attract visitors who are sometimes even more valuable than those who come from search engines. Social networks give the opportunity to attract people who are already motivated to buy your product.
