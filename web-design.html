Web Hosting We Use

Our web design Dubai company is very careful in choosing a web hosting. The success of SEO Dubai services directly depends on the speed of the web server and the connection speed. Cheap American companies cannot cope with these tasks.

The best option is a private VPS on Digital Ocean or Amazon. Data centers must be located in Europe. The connection speed between Dubai and Europe is high. The websites load pretty quickly. You can also use European or Russian shared hosting. They are suitable for small websites.

To make the website load speed even faster, you can connect Cloudflare. This service has data centers scattered all over the world. It caches the data and gives it to you from the nearest one.
